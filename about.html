About Pexel Pulse

Our story, our mission, and the people who make the magic happen.

Our Story

Pexel Pulse was born from a simple idea: Tembisa deserves world-class gaming and learning spaces too. Our founder grew up in Tembisa and knew firsthand that young people here have the same passion for gaming and technology as anyone else — they just lacked the right environment.

We opened our doors in 2023 with a small selection of gaming stations and a big dream. Today, Pexel Pulse is the most loved gaming studio in Tembisa, offering not just gaming, but internet access, a vending cafe for snacks and hot drinks, and weekend coding and robotics classes for kids who want to build the future.

We are more than a business — we are a community hub. Every rand spent at Pexel Pulse goes back into the local economy and into creating opportunities for young people in our township.

Our Mission & Values

What drives us every single day.

Our Mission

To provide Tembisa with access to premium gaming, technology, and digital education in a safe, inclusive, and affordable space.

Innovation

We constantly update our equipment and curriculum so our community always has access to the latest technology and gaming experiences.

Inclusion

Everyone is welcome at Pexel Pulse — regardless of age, gender, or background. We believe technology should be for everyone.

Community

We reinvest in Tembisa by hiring local staff, supporting local events, and running free public workshops every quarter.
