About Pexel Pulse

How we started and who works here.

Our Story

Pexel Pulse started in 2023. Sipho grew up in Tembisa and wanted a proper place to game and learn tech, not just a few chairs in the corner of a shop.

We began with a handful of consoles and PCs. Now we run 20+ stations, an internet cafe, Pulse Cafe for snacks and drinks, and Saturday and Sunday classes for kids.

Most of our staff live nearby. We try to keep prices fair and hire locally where we can.
