FAQs

Common questions answered.

Do I need to book in advance?

For gaming and internet access, walk-ins are welcome. For kids academy classes, please book in advance as spots fill up fast.

What age is the kids academy for?

Coding classes are open to ages 7–17. Robotics classes are for ages 9–17. All levels of experience are welcome.

Can I have a birthday party here?

Yes! We offer birthday party packages for kids and adults. Contact us to discuss options and pricing tailored to your group.

Is there parking available?

Yes, there is free parking available outside our venue in Tembisa. Our location is easily accessible by taxi and public transport too.
