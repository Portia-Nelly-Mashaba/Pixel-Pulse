FAQs

Stuff people ask a lot.

Do I need to book in advance?

Gaming and internet: walk in. Kids classes: book first, especially weekends.

What age are the kids classes?

Coding: 7 to 17. Robotics: 9 to 17. Beginners are fine.

Can I have a birthday party here?

Yes. Parties for kids and adults. Call us for dates and a price for your group size.

Is there parking available?

Free parking outside. Easy to reach by taxi or kombi from around Tembisa.
