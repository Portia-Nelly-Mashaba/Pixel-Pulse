What We Offer

Gaming, internet, food, and classes for kids.

Gaming Studio

PS5, Xbox, Switch, gaming PCs and VR. Pay by the hour or grab a longer session deal.

Internet Cafe

Fibre internet on our PCs. Print, scan, type CVs, school work, or just browse the web.

Pulse Cafe

Coffee, cold drinks, chips, noodles and toasted sandwiches while you wait your turn or use a PC.

Coding Classes

Coding for kids 7 to 17 on weekends. Scratch for beginners, then HTML and Python as they get older.

Robotics Classes

LEGO Mindstorms and Arduino on weekends. Kids build small robots and learn how they work.
